Pyramidle

How to Play

Guess any word from length 4 to 11 to win.

Guesses 1 to 3 show clues for word of length 4, if 4th guess is incorrect the clues of already guessed words will update for the word of subsequent length

Example

The letter R is in the next word and in the correct spot.

The letters C and A are in the next word but in the wrong spot.

The letters E and M are not in the next word.

Fisrt 3 guesses show clues for the word with length 4.

After guessing the wrong word all the clues are changed to match the next word.
